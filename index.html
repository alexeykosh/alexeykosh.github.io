I am a PhD student at CRPN, Aix-Marseille University and Institut Jean Nicod, ENS-PSL, where I am supervised by Isabelle Dautriche and Olivier Morin. My research focuses on understanding why languages are the way they are. I rely on computational modeling, experimental studies, and empirical data to explain why languages seem to be tailored for efficient communication.
